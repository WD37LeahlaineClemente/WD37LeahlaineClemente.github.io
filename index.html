Here’s Our Story

It all started in year 1991, when Victoria Madrigal was offered to cook for breakfast and lunch of 50 to 100 persons daily in a cargo company.
Then, she started a small carenderia and ihaw-ihaw. She also dreamt of having a catering business, but it was not push through.

Years passed, her daughter Leahlaine frequently requested to handle activities serving 100 to 400 persons. Later on, Leahlaine
enjoyed doing the event planning and coordinating, as well as, managing the catering. In no time, they decided to register
business license for MACES Event Solution.

Since then, through word-of-mouth advertising, the services grew dramatically; serving a wide variety of dishes that
compliments good quality service with the value of money, making it all in a worry-free setting.

Today, MACES have catered many occasions from small gatherings to grand, managed by Leahlaine Clemente,
a certified Event Management Expert. If you want a catering company that will go above and beyond the
call of duty to make your event a success, regardless of the size or the location, look no further.
